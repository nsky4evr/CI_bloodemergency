Blood Emergency Nepal is a must have app at medical emergencies.

Find reliable blood donors right away from your phone's contact list. Find which of your family members and friends are your blood match.

Contact and Navigate the nearest Hospitals, Blood-banks, Pharmacies & Blood donation events using the application.

Easy to use features

Application is multifunctional and simple to use as it's your life saver at the time of emergencies.

Hospital/Blood-bank

Find the nearest Hospitals, blood-banks from your current location. Communicate instantly to those hospitals at emergencies.

Navigate

Find the nearest route to those hospitals, blood-banks, organizations, pharmacies and blood donation events using your app.

Easy registration

Immediates

Bring all your immediate contacts to a common place to ease your search. We help you keep your immediates right at your finger tip.
